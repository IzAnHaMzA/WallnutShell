Our Story

Walnut Shell: Crafted Around Quality

A premium truffle supplier focused on excellence, freshness, and trusted sourcing relationships.

Dedicated to Gourmet Standards

Walnut Shell was founded with a clear purpose: to provide carefully sourced truffles that meet the expectations of high-end kitchens and discerning buyers. Every order is handled with attention to freshness, handling quality, and communication.

Our core offerings include Black Truffle and White Truffle, both recognized for their culinary value and distinctive aroma. As natural products, truffles are seasonal by nature, and availability depends on harvest periods. This is why our ordering model is contact-driven, ensuring each customer receives current availability details directly.

Freshness and Responsible Sourcing

We work with sourcing channels that prioritize quality and product integrity. Walnut Shell emphasizes responsible handling, clean logistics, and timely delivery coordination so clients receive truffles in excellent condition.
